Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, phone number, educational background, location, and profile details. We also collect usage data such as login times and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match students with suitable tutors.
  • To process payments and track commissions.
  • To send notifications about your tuition activities.
  • To improve platform features and user experience.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your profile information (name, subjects, location) is visible to other registered users on the platform for the purpose of tutor-student matching. Payment details are processed securely and are not shared with third parties beyond what is required for transaction processing.

4. Phone Number & OTP

Your phone number is used for account verification via OTP and for contact purposes within the platform. We do not share your phone number with external advertisers.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. All data is stored on secured servers with encrypted connections.

6. Guru AI

When you interact with Guru AI, your messages and anonymised tutor/post data are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not store Guru AI conversation history. Please do not share sensitive personal information in AI chats.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies for session management and to remember your theme preference. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Account deletion will remove your profile and associated data from our active systems.

9. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact us at privacy@tuitionmedia.com
